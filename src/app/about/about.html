About BookSearch

BookSearch is a web application that allows you to search for books by title and save your favourites

Each book has its own card with the books name, author, publish year and image

Search results are pulled from the Open Library API. Any book you save is stored in a MongoDB database and will appear on your Favourites page

You can then delete books from your database, also deleting them from the MongoDB
